  • No partner action required
  • Message: "standardising existing terms"
  • Trigger: Unlikely. Justin on standby for hotel partner media queries.
  • Engagement: Brief Ludivine + Justin before rollout.

Phase 1b: Medium PR Risk

  • Schema type change could confuse connected partners
  • Reconciliation discrepancies may generate complaints
  • Triggers: Provider complaints, billing disputes
  • Engagement: Brief Kyle + Justin before 60-day window. Ludivine coordinates.

  • Double-charging travellers is #1 reputational risk
  • 170k+ properties, front desk noise
  • Triggers: Media reports, partner criticism, social media, regulatory inquiry
  • Engagement: Full PR team. Crisis playbook co-developed with Anne. Justin + Kyle on active standby from certification.
